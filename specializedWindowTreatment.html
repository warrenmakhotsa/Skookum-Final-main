Our Available Options

Dream Blinds

Dream blind also known as "Allusion Blinds" refer to a specific type of window treatment that combines the features of sheer curtains and traditional horizontal blinds. They offer a unique blend of functionality and style, creating an elegant look that is versatile and practical. Dream blinds are made from layers of sheer fabric with horizontal, fabric vanes that move between the two layers. This design creates a soft, flowing appearance while allowing you to control the amount of light and privacy in a room. The vanes can tilt open or closed, providing different levels of light control and a beautiful, layered effect.

Skylight Blinds

Skylight blinds are specifically made for windows that are set into a sloped or angled ceiling, as opposed to standard vertical or horizontal windows. They are designed to fit snugly and securely over skylight windows, providing a stylish and functional way to control light, temperature, and privacy.

Skylight Blinds Brochure

Outdoor Blinds

Outdoor blinds are window coverings specifically made for use in exterior spaces. They can be installed on patios, porches, balconies, decks, or any other outdoor structure to block the sun, protect against the elements, and create a private outdoor area. They are built from durable, weather-resistant materials and come in various styles and mechanisms to suit different needs.

Outdoor Blinds Brochure

Panel Blinds

Panel blinds, also known as panel track blinds or sliding panel blinds, are a stylish and practical option for covering large windows, sliding doors, and room dividers. They provide a sleek and contemporary look that can add elegance to modern and minimalist interiors

Roman Blinds

Roman blinds are a popular and stylish window treatment that combine the functionality of traditional blinds with the elegance of fabric drapery. They are known for their ability to add a touch of sophistication and warmth to a room while providing effective light control and privacy

Honeycomb Blinds

Honeycomb blinds, also known as cellular shades, are a popular type of window treatment that stands out for their unique construction and practical benefits. Their design is not only visually appealing but also highly functional, offering superior insulation, energy efficiency, and light control.
