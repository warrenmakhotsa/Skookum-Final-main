Our Available Options

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds are window treatments consisting of individual slats or vanes that hang vertically from a headrail at the top of a window. These slats can be adjusted to control light and privacy by rotating them or drawing them to the side.

Vertical Blinds Brochure

Roller Blinds

Roller blinds are window coverings made from a single piece of fabric that rolls up and down over a window via a mechanism usually operated by a cord, chain, or motor. They are a popular choice for window treatments due to their simplicity, versatility, and effectiveness in controlling light and privacy.

Roller Blinds Brochure

Venetian Blinds

Venetian blinds are a type of window covering made of horizontal slats that can be adjusted to control the amount of light and privacy in a room. They are typically made from materials such as aluminum, wood, or faux wood and are a popular choice for homes and offices due to their versatility and sleek appearance..

Venetian Blinds Brochure

Zebra Blinds

Zebra blinds, also known as vision blinds or day and night blinds, are a type of window treatment that combines the features of both roller blinds and venetian blinds. They consist of alternating translucent and opaque horizontal fabric strips that overlap to create a zebra-stripe pattern when the blinds are adjusted. This design allows for flexible control of light and privacy.

Sunscreen Blinds

These window coverings are made from specially engineered fabric that helps to prevent glare, blockout harmful UV rays and offer unparalleled privacy without compromising on light.

Blackout Blinds

These are great for boardrooms or meeting rooms because they blockout light and allow you to make presentations without distractions.
